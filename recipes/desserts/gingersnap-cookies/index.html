Ingredients

3/4 c Crisco butter flavored 1 c sugar 1 egg 1/4 c molasses 2 tsp. baking soda 2 c flour 1/2 tsp. cloves 1/2 heaping tsp. ginger 1 heaping tsp. cinnamon 1/2 tsp. salt

Directions

Put shortening and sugar in mixer and mix them together. Add egg and beat till pale yellow. Add molasses and mix till incorporated. In a separate bowl, mix dry ingredients together. Pour dry ingredients into mixer and mix well. Roll into balls, being careful not to compress dough, and gently coat in sugar. Bake on greased pan. 375Â° for 8-10 minutes.
