Ingredients

  • 3/4 c Crisco Butter flavored
  • 1 c Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 c molasses

Dry Ingredients

  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 c flour
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 1/2 heaping tsp ginger
  • 1 heaping tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Put Crisco Butter and sugar in mixer and mix them together.
  2. Add egg and beat till pale yellow.
  3. Add molasses and mix till incorporated.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix dry ingredients together.
  5. Pour dry ingredients into mixer and mix well.
  6. Roll into balls, being careful not to compress dough, and gently coat in sugar.
  7. Bake on greased pan at 375Â° for 8-10 minutes.
