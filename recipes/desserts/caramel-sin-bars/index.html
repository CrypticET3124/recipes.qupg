Ingredients

Caramel Filling:

  • 1 stick (110g) butter
  • 32 Kraft caramel's or 1 1/3 cup (320ml) Kraft caramel bits
  • 1 can (397g) sweetened condensed Milk

Crust:

  • 1 pkg yellow cake mix
  • 1 cup (237ml) white chocolate chips
  • 2 cup (473ml) chocolate chips
  • 1 Heath bar crushed or crushed home made toffee (optional)
  • 1/2 cup (118ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs

Directions

  1. In sauce pan, combine caramel filling ingredients and stir until melted. Stir constantly to avoid scorching.
  2. Mix crust ingredients together in a mixer.
  3. Preheat oven 350 F (177 C). Grease a 13x9 inch (33x23x5cm) baking pan. Press 3/4's crust mix into pan. Cook for 15 minutes.
  4. Cool on cooling rack 10 min.
  5. Pour Caramel mixture onto cake. Drop remaining dough by spoonfuls over caramel covering. Bake for another 20-25 minutes. Edges should be golden. Cool 40 min. Cover and cool in fridge before eating or eat it warm!

Notes

Golden around the edges refers to the caramel bubbling up around it. Waiting for the cake to brown will overcook the caramel into a chewy, yet yummy consistency that could easily pull the fillings from your teeth.
For nut allergies, use nut free ingredients and omit Heath Bar.
