Ingredients

  • 8 oz of chicken broth
  • 6-8 lbs of pork roast, trimmed and cut into several pieces
  • 1/4 cup Kosher salt
  • Up to 1 bottle of Hickory flavored liquid smoke

Directions

  1. Put the chicken broth in the instant pot.
  2. With each chunk of meat, sprinkle salt and liquid smoke on a large flat side. Place the salted side down in the instant pot.
  3. Sprinkle the other side with salt and liquid smoke.
  4. Repeat until all the meat is in the pot. Seal it up and set it to pressure cook for 2 hours.
  5. When cooking is done, release the pressure. Then use a slotted spoon to remove chunks.
  6. Shred the meat and remove any remaining fat or connective tissue.
  7. Add some of the juices to shredded meat. Serve.
  8. Optionally, you can add more liquid smoke or salt to taste.

Notes

Cutting the meat into chunks helps ensure that the flavors get into every part.
