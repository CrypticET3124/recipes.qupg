Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp minced shallots
  • 1 1/2 tbsp honey, raw for Paleo
  • 1/2 tbsp poppy seeds

Directions

  1. Place all the dressing ingredients in a small jar and shake.
